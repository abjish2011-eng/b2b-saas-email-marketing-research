# Newsletter / Email Marketing for B2B SaaS Research

This repository collects recent public content from experts who actively work in newsletter strategy, email marketing, B2B SaaS growth.

## What I Collected

- `research/sources.md` - expert list with links, dates, and short notes
- `research/linkedin-posts/` - LinkedIn posts organized by author and newsletter
- `research/youtube-transcripts/` - YouTube transcripts organized by author and newsletter
- `research/other/` - additional public materials such as Substack, X, Apple Podcast, and supporting notes

## Why These Experts

I chose people who:

- Work directly on newsletter, email for B2B SaaS
- Have public, recent content we can reference
- Share practical advice, not just theory
- Represent a mix of platform operators, creators, and practitioners

Many companies are #1 on a benchmark they crafted. We wanted to do something different.

We partnered with micro1 to create an independently audited benchmark measuring document extraction performance on long, complex documents. Reducto had no role in selecting the test documents or modifying the ground truth.

The benchmark, LongExtractBench, tested 225 real documents averaging 358 pages each, with roughly 88.7K ground truth fields. micro1 evaluated frontier models at max reasoning and dedicated document processing platforms at their strongest configurations.

What emerged was a clear pattern: most solutions face a precision/recall tradeoff. Frontier models actually performed well on precision for completed documents, hitting 90%+ scores that surpassed dedicated extraction services. But recall was a different story — even on the easier subset of commonly completed documents, models fell below 80%, repeatedly dropping rows and important values.

Reducto's Deep Extract was the only solution that was both complete and accurate across the full test set: 225/225 documents completed, 99%+ precision and recall, zero failures.

That last point matters. Supporting real-world enterprise use cases requires more than accuracy on paper — pipelines need to be reliable and complete, every time. This benchmark reflects the work our team has put into making Deep Extract production-ready.

We see this as a way to surface the silent failures that exist outside of marketing content, but the best evaluation will always be on your own data.
